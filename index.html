About SPARC

The Southeastern Prevention and Addiction Recovery Resource Center (SPARC) implements a regional hub and spoke partnership model to coordinate substance abuse prevention and recovery services across six North Carolina counties: Robeson, Cumberland, Richmond, Scotland, Columbus, and Bladen.
